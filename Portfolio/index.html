About

Hi there! I’m Bella Pope, a sophomore Graphic Design and Spanish major at the University of Wisconsin–Eau Claire. I’m seeking opportunities in social media, logo design, brand design, and website design. I’m passionate about combining visual communication and language to create clear, meaningful design work, and I also plan to pursue a Linguistics certificate to further connect my interests in design and language. At school, I’ve been building skills in typography, layout, and digital design while continuing to grow my Spanish fluency and cultural understanding. I’ve also had pieces shown in the Foster Art Gallery, which has been a meaningful part of my development as a designer and has encouraged me to keep refining my creative voice and portfolio. My goal is to grow as a designer who creates thoughtful, effective visuals and digital experiences that communicate ideas clearly and creatively.
